## 2026-W16 (2026-04-13 to 2026-04-19)

**BLUF.** EDPB's April plenary produced three consequential outputs: draft
scientific-research guidelines, an accelerated anonymisation track, and a
DPIA template targeting GDPR compliance cost; Commissioner Kubilius used
the SEDE structured dialogue to reiterate defence as the Commission's
top priority. Operational load for EU CSIRTs was dominated by a Fortinet
cluster (FortiAnalyzer/Manager, FortiSandbox) coordinated across ANSSI
and NCSC-NL, plus Cisco ISE, Splunk Cloud, and SAP HANA/S/4 patches.

### Operational advisories

Le CERT-FR a publié son Bulletin d'actualité CERTFR-2026-ACT-017
(bulletin), recensant les vulnérabilités significatives de la semaine
écoulée, dont Fortinet FortiClientEMS (CVE-2026-35616, CVSS 9.8,
exploitée, contournement de politique de sécurité et exécution de code
à distance) et Adobe Acrobat Reader (CVE-2026-34621, CVSS 8.6,
exploitée). https://www.cert.ssi.gouv.fr/actualite/CERTFR-2026-ACT-017/
retrieved 2026-04-16T18:29:54Z.

CERT-FR advisory CERTFR-2026-AVI-0440 covers multiple vulnerabilities
in Fortinet FortiAnalyzer, FortiAnalyzer Cloud, FortiManager, and
FortiManager Cloud, enumerating risks including SQL injection, SSRF,
XSS, heap-based overflow, privilege escalation, and unauthenticated
remote code execution. Fixed versions include FortiAnalyzer Cloud 7.6.5
and 7.4.9. https://www.cert.ssi.gouv.fr/avis/CERTFR-2026-AVI-0440/
retrieved 2026-04-16T18:29:55Z.

NCSC-NL beveiligingsadvies NCSC-2026-0121 (security advisory) addresses
Fortinet FortiSandbox (on-premises and Cloud), flagging two
Fortinet-classified critical flaws (CVE-2026-39813 OS command injection
and CVE-2026-39808 path traversal in the JRPC API) enabling
unauthenticated remote code execution via crafted HTTP requests. NCSC-NL
rates impact Medium/High. https://advisories.ncsc.nl/2026/ncsc-2026-0121.html
retrieved 2026-04-16T18:29:55Z.

CERT-FR advisory CERTFR-2026-AVI-0434 covers SAP product vulnerabilities
affecting Business Analytics/Content Management (S4HCMRXX and SAP_HRRXX),
Business Planning and Consolidation / Business Warehouse on HANA, with
risks spanning confidentiality compromise, policy bypass, denial of
service, RCE, XSS, and SQLi; April SAP Security Patch Day cluster.
https://www.cert.ssi.gouv.fr/avis/CERTFR-2026-AVI-0434/ retrieved
2026-04-16T18:29:55Z.

CERT-FR advisory CERTFR-2026-AVI-0451 covers Cisco ISE and ISE-PIC
versions 3.2 through 3.5; ISE 3.3 requires Patch 8 and 11, 3.4 requires
Patch 4 and 6, 3.5 requires Patch 3. Risks: security-policy bypass and
remote arbitrary code execution; relevant for EU-27 SOC and identity
deployments under NIS2 Article 21 scope.
https://www.cert.ssi.gouv.fr/avis/CERTFR-2026-AVI-0451/ retrieved
2026-04-16T18:30:58Z.

CERT-FR advisory CERTFR-2026-AVI-0450 covers Splunk Cloud Platform,
with affected versions 10.0.2503 (fixed 10.0.2503.13), 10.1.2507.x
(fixed 10.1.2507.20), 10.2.2510.x (fixed 10.2.2510.10), and

confidentiality compromise, security-policy bypass, and remote

### Data protection

EDPB plenary in Brussels on 16 April 2026 adopted Guidelines on the
processing of personal data for scientific research purposes, created a
dedicated team to accelerate finalisation of the anonymisation
guidelines, and adopted two opinions approving the Europrivacy
certification criteria as European Data Protection Seals, one of which
is approved as a tool for international transfers.
https://www.edpb.europa.eu/news/news/2026/edpb-brings-clarity-data-processing-scientific-research-speeds-finalisation_en
retrieved 2026-04-16T18:30:41Z.

EDPB plenary on 14 April 2026 adopted a Data Protection Impact
Assessment (DPIA) template aligned with the Board's Helsinki Statement
on easing GDPR compliance. The template is accompanied by an explainer
document and is intended to harmonise DPIA structure, evidence, and
reporting across controllers EU-wide.
https://www.edpb.europa.eu/news/news/2026/enhancing-compliance-and-consistency-edpb-adopts-dpia-template_en
retrieved 2026-04-16T18:30:41Z.
